Application Data

The Atlantic hurricane database and the Northeast and North Central Pacific hurricane database were obtained from the National Hurricane Center webpage. These databases are in a comma-delimited text format. The dataset format information is available in the following link.

Data fields
FIELD DESCRIPTION VALUES
name Hurricane Name Given by dataset. UNNAMED for old registers.
basin Atlantic or North Pacific basin
id Unique identifier Created from Basin + Cyclone Number
timestamp: year Year event Interval: [1851-2014]
timestamp: month Month event
timestamp: day Day event
timestamp: hour Hour event
timestamp: minute Minute event
maxwind Maximum sustained wind (in knots)
minpressure Minimum Pressure (int millibars)
latitude Latitude
longitude Longitude
l Record identifier for event
  • C – Closest approach to a coast, not followed by a landfall
  • G – Genesis
  • I – An intensity peak in terms of both pressure and wind
  • L – Landfall (center of system crossing a coastline)
  • P – Minimum in central pressure
  • R – Provides additional detail on the intensity of the cyclone when rapid changes are underway
  • S – Change of status of the system
  • T – Provides additional detail on the track (position) of the cyclone
  • W – Maximum sustained wind speed
  • -999 - no data
ts Status of system
  • TD – Tropical cyclone of tropical depression intensity (< 34 knots)
  • TS – Tropical cyclone of tropical storm intensity (34-63 knots)
  • HU – Tropical cyclone of hurricane intensity (> 64 knots)
  • EX – Extratropical cyclone (of any intensity)
  • SD – Subtropical cyclone of subtropical depression intensity (< 34 knots)
  • SS – Subtropical cyclone of subtropical storm intensity (> 34 knots)
  • LO – A low that is neither a tropical cyclone, a subtropical cyclone, nor an extratropical cyclone (of any intensity)
  • WV – Tropical Wave (of any intensity)
  • DB – Disturbance (of any intensity)
  • -999 - no data
q34: ne 34kt wind radii extent in northeastern quadrant In nautical miles. -999 is no data exists.
q34: se 34kt wind radii extent in southeastern quadrant In nautical miles. -999 is no data exists.
q34: sw 34kt wind radii extent in northwestern quadrant In nautical miles. -999 is no data exists.
q34: nw 34kt wind radii extent in northwestern quadrant In nautical miles. -999 is no data exists.
q50: ne 50kt wind radii extent in northeastern quadrant In nautical miles. -999 is no data exists.
q50: se 50kt wind radii extent in southeastern quadrant In nautical miles. -999 is no data exists.
q50: sw 34kt wind radii extent in southwestern quadrant In nautical miles. -999 is no data exists.
q50: nw 34kt wind radii extent in northwestern quadrant In nautical miles. -999 is no data exists.
q64: ne 64kt wind radii extent in northeastern quadrant In nautical miles. -999 is no data exists.
q64: se 64kt wind radii extent in southeastern quadrant In nautical miles. -999 is no data exists.
q64: sw 64kt wind radii extent in southwestern quadrant In nautical miles. -999 is no data exists.
q64: nw 64kt wind radii extent in northwestern quadrant In nautical miles. -999 is no data exists.

Parsing

The NHC text datasets were used to create a single GeoJSON file. A C# custom development was used for this task.
