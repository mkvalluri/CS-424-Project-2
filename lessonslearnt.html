Lessons Learnt

  • The heatmap information could have avoided areas in the sea or focus just on land and areas near shores.
  • The hurricane animation could have left the path until the hurricane reaches its final date. (ie. Proj Link)
  • Missing a button to select or deselect all in the hurricanes name container (ie. Proj Link)
  • Use slider or buttons for increasing frame rate (ie. Proj Link)
  • The overall color theme could have been better
